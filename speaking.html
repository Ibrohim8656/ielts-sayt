Speaking Mock Test

Read the questions aloud and record your answers to practice.

Part 1: Introduction and Interview

  • What is your full name?
  • Where are you from originally?
  • Do you work or are you a student?
  • What do you like most about your hometown?

Part 2: Long Turn

Describe a website that you often use.

You should say:

  • what it is
  • how often you use it
  • what you use it for

and explain why this website is important or useful for you.

Part 3: Discussion

  • How has the internet affected the way we communicate?
  • Do you think people rely too much on technology these days?
  • What are the advantages and disadvantages of online shopping compared to physical stores?
